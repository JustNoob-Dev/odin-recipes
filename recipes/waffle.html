Description

These waffles turn out lovely and crispy. Perfect for any day of the week!

Steps

  1. Preheat waffle iron
  2. Combine flour, sugar, baking powder, and salt
  3. Separate egg whites and yolks
  4. Whisk egg whites until stiff peaks form
  5. Whisk egg yolks, milk, and butter
  6. Combine wet and dry ingredients
  7. Fold in egg whites
  8. Cook in waffle iron until golden brown
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!
