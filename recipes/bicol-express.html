Description

Spicy, savory, and creamy pork stew from the Philippines

The dish is known for its rich, umami flavor and slight pinkish hue.

Steps

  1. Heat oil in a pan
  2. Saute garlic, onion, and ginger
  3. Add pork and cook until brown
  4. Season with shrimp paste and chili
  5. Add coconut milk and simmer
  6. Season with salt and pepper
  7. Serve hot and enjoy!
